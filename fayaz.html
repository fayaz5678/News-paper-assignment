Pimento cheese and Heirloom Tomato Tart with Ritz Cracker crust

Makes 1 (9-inch) tart

Ingredients:

    2 sleeves Ritz crackers (about 7.5 ounces or 13/4 cup crushed cracker)
    1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
    10 tablespoons butter, melted

Directions:

    preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrehei
    add crackers to bowl of food processor process until crackers are a fine crumb add parmesan cheese, melted butter and 1/2 teaspoon koshersalt process until evenly combined and mixture holds together when pressed between fingers if it doesnt hold together, add an additional tablespoon of melted butter
